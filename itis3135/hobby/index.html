What is Spotify?

  • To understand what spotify wrapped works, you need to know about spotify first.
  • Spotify offers music and podcasts on their app for users to listen to.
  • Users can create playlists and follow thier favorite artist.

What is Spotify Wrapped?

  • Spotify Wrapped started in 2016 at the end of the year.
  • It allowed the users of Spotify to view the statistics of thier music data.
  • Each account has different data that is unique to that user.
  • Spotify collects data from January 1 and October 31 to create one's Spotify Wrapped.

Release


Your Top Songs 2021 Playlist:

Each year in December, Spotify releases thier Spotify Wrapped. Along with the recap, they also create a unique playlist for that user. The playlist contains all of the top songs that user listened to that year.

Your Artist Revealed Playlist:

Spotify also releases another playlist named, "Your Artist Revealed Playlist". This playlist provides a mix of songs and other from the top artists of the user. This can include songs and small comments from the artists and spotify.

How to Find Your Spotify Wrapped

Steps:

  1. Open up Spotify on Mobile
  2. Select the Home icon from the bottom toolbar
  3. Click "#SPOTIFYWRAPPED" and "Your 2021 in review"

If you would like to locate the playlists, you can go to https://open.spotify.com/genre/2021-page This link can be opened on desktop or mobile and brings you to the webpage to find the 2021 playlists that spotify creates.

Importance


Spotify Wrapped has been collecting data from users and creating something they think users can benefit from. Users can now go back and look at what thier music tastes used to be like. They can then see a timeline of how they may have evolved. They can come to terms with a better understanding of how they define themselves.

Not only can they see their own music tastes, users share thier tastes with others as well. People can understand another better with thier taste in music.
