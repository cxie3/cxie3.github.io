Clean Up

Taking care of succluent plants can be pretty time consuming, but here are some items that can make it easier:

  • Blower: There will be excess dirt on your plants, but a blower can remove them quickly.

  • Brush: For all of the hard to get places that the blower can't do, you can use the brush to sweep them away.

  • Tweezer: Succulent plants are small and to pluck dead parts out, it is easier to use tweezers.
