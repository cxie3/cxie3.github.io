Project Design

Project overview:

  • Application and Purpose:
    - This website is going to be used to show and inform people on how to take care of succulent plants.
  • Intended Users:
    - Those who have succulent plants or are interested in succulent plants and would like a little more information on them can browse the website.
  • Overview of Content:
    - The website will contain information on how to take care of the plants and will contain various pictures. Home page will give an overview, how to take care will explain the watering techniques and such and will branch off to temperature and clean up page. There will also be a page on pots and just pictures of succulent plants.
